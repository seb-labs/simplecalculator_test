Simple Calculator is a small Android calculator built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose.

It is designed for quick everyday use and keeps the interface clean and easy to read.

Features:
- addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
- offline-only operation
- no ads, tracking, or Google Play services
- simple, modern Compose UI

The app is intended to stay easy to build from source and suitable for F-Droid-style publication.
